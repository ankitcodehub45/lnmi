Courses We Offer

we are offering you six type of proffesionalscourses.Course offerings are going to go down.

BCA

BCA or Bachelors of Computer Applications is a three-year undergraduate course which is designed to make students aware of the basic aspects related to computers and make them proficient in the same. The course is so organized within the span of three years so as to provide deep insights of the major computer domains in a step-by-step manner.

BBA

Bachelors in Business Administration, that is to say, BBA course is a three-year UG degree programme for aspirants who wish to acquire the knowledge of functional aspects of a company and their correlations. Candidates can also pursue the academic knowledge in a particular specialisation which further helps in attainment of knowledge within the bracket of the specific area.

MCA

It is a 2-year professional postgraduate course that deals with the various aspects of computer science and applications. The MCA scope is vast considering the wide use of computers across industries Freshers of the MCA can apply for a diversity of designations such as System Administrators, Computer Scientist, Software Developer.

MBA

It is a 2-year post-graduate courses in India and abroad. For most of the managerial level jobs across domains and sectors an MBA degree is a necessity which is why, nowadays a large number of BTech, BBA, BCom, BA, BSc, BCA graduates opt for MBA in post graduation

MBA-IB

It is a 2 year full-time postgraduation program that imparts extensive knowledge in the field of Business and Management and equips students with skills required to get adopted to the multidisciplinary nature of the global market.

MBA-MHRM

MBA MHRM is a 2-year postgraduate degree course opted by students who want to pursue a successful career in the human resource management field.Students must complete their graduation in Commerce related stream such as BCom or BBA with a minimum score of 55% aggregate marks or equivalent CGPA from a recognised college or university.
